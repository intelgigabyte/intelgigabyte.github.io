📖 General Installation Guide

To ensure a smooth custom ROM installation process, please read the following guide carefully. Note that installing custom ROMs can be risky if not done correctly.

Prerequisites:

  • Samsung Galaxy A71 4G phone (model a71) with an unlocked Bootloader.
  • ADB and Fastboot Drivers installed on your computer.
  • All important data on your phone has been backed up.
  • Phone battery is fully charged (at least 80%).

Basic Installation Steps:

  1. Download the appropriate ROM and Recovery (TWRP) for your device from the links above.
  2. Flash TWRP Recovery to your phone using Odin (on Windows) or via ADB/Fastboot (on Linux/macOS).
  3. Reboot your phone into TWRP Recovery.
  4. Perform a Wipe Data/Factory Reset (formatting data is recommended to avoid encryption issues).
  5. Flash the downloaded ROM. Depending on the ROM, you might need to flash GApps (Google Apps) and Magisk (for root) additionally.
  6. Reboot system and complete the initial setup process.

Important Note: Each ROM and Android version may have specific requirements or installation steps. Always refer to the detailed instructions on the ROM page or my Telegram group before proceeding.
